About Me

My name is Jobany Giraldo, and I live in Armenia, Quindio. I am a student at SENA. Through this blog, I want to share all my learning and knowledge, not only about English but also about the technologies and skills I am acquiring.

My hobbies include swimming, enjoying the sea, traveling to new places. As a hobby, I like fixing broken things, listening to music, and dancing salsa.
